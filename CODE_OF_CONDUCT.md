# Code of Conduct

GovLens follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) v2.1.

## In short

- **Be respectful.** Disagree on ideas, not people.
- **Be welcoming.** New contributors of any background, skill level, or language proficiency are explicitly welcome.
- **Assume good intent.** Most miscommunication is just that — miscommunication.
- **Keep it about the work.** Off-topic political, religious, or personal arguments don't belong here.

## What's not OK

- Insults, slurs, harassment of any kind
- Personal attacks
- Publishing others' private info without consent
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Sustained disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

The maintainer ([@sinhaankur](https://github.com/sinhaankur)) handles enforcement. Reports go to the email on the GitHub profile. All reports are reviewed; the reporter's identity is kept confidential.

Consequences scale with the violation: warning → temporary ban → permanent ban. The maintainer's call is final but they will explain reasoning when asked.

## Scope

Applies in all GovLens spaces — Issues, PRs, Discussions, Discord/Slack if we add them later, and any in-person events organised under the GovLens name.
